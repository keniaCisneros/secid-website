About Us

SECiD (Sociedad de Egresados en Ciencia de Datos) is a dynamic and forward-thinking professional network exclusively dedicated to alumni from the data science programs at the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM). Founded by passionate data professionals who recognized the transformative potential of community and continuous learning, our organization serves as a critical bridge between academic excellence and the rapidly evolving technological landscape.

Our mission extends far beyond traditional alumni networks. We are committed to creating a comprehensive ecosystem that supports data science professionals throughout their career journey, from recent graduates to seasoned industry experts. By fostering meaningful connections, facilitating knowledge exchange, and providing strategic resources, we aim to elevate the professional trajectory of each member while collectively advancing the field of data science in Mexico and beyond.

Through a multifaceted approach, SECiD offers a rich array of opportunities including exclusive networking events, cutting-edge workshops, mentorship programs, and a specialized job board that connects top-tier talent with innovative organizations. We believe in the power of collaborative learning and professional development, understanding that the most significant breakthroughs emerge from sharing diverse perspectives and collective expertise.

Our community is not just about individual advancement but about creating a robust, supportive network that represents the highest standards of technical excellence, ethical practice, and innovative thinking. We celebrate the unique strengths of UNAM's data science alumni and work tirelessly to ensure that our members are not just participants, but leaders and pioneers in the global data science ecosystem.

As the technological landscape continues to evolve at an unprecedented pace, SECiD remains dedicated to providing our members with the tools, connections, and insights necessary to stay at the forefront of innovation, adapt to emerging trends, and make meaningful contributions to their organizations and society at large.
